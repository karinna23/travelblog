What is Vessel Traffic Management System?

The Subic Bay Freeport has introduced a new Vessel Traffic Management System (VTMS) to modernize and enhance maritime operations. This upgraded system, initiated by the Subic Bay Metropolitan Authority (SBMA), is designed to improve efficiency, safety, and security at the Port of Subic, addressing limitations in radar coverage from the previous 15-year-old system. VTMS is a technology-driven system used in ports to monitor and manage ship movements within the harbor and nearby waters. It utilizes radar, AIS (Automatic Identification Systems), VHF radios, CCTVs, and specialized software to*track vessels, detect traffic, and assist in decision-making for safe port operations. The new Subic VTMS includes updated radar stations, improved hardware and software, visual surveillance, and a Port Management System for handling administrative tasks like billing and coordination with service providers.
