What is IVET?

An Industrial Visit and Educational Tour (IVET) is a learning activity where students visit companies, organizations, or significant sites related to their field of study. The goal is to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world applications.

During the visit, students gain firsthand insights into how industries operate, explore new technologies, understand workflows, and observe professional environments. It enhances their practical understanding, encourages career exploration, and promotes experiential learning outside the classroom.

BSIT TOUR

The purpose of the educational tour was to provide students with practical exposure to real-world industry environments, bridging the gap between academic learning and professional application. Organized by the faculty of the Western Mindanao State University - College of Computing Studies, the tour aimed to enhance our understanding of how Information Technology concepts are applied in actual workplace settings.

As the pioneering batch to experience this initiative, the tour served as a preparatory step before our On-the-Job Training (OJT), which is the final requirement prior to graduation. This opportunity not only allowed us to observe industry practices firsthand but also helped us gain valuable insights that will guide us as we transition from students to future IT professionals.
